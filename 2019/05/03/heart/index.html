Confide my heart

2019-05-03
  • IDEA
Hi everyone. It is very happy for me that you can browse my articles. Some of the following are the current status and feelings of my life.

I’m a senior 2 student now,What I have to do every day is to learn.Unfortunately,Although I study hard every day, my grades is still not so good.Facing the approaching college entrance examination,I’m so desperate.Sometimes I’m so confused, can I really go to college? In addition to these, I still have a bunch of troubles in my life.They are too heavy, so I can’t breathe.But I still got a little happiness, just a few, but it’s enough.My high school life, classmates, friends, they brought me a lot of happiness, making my attitude towards life less negative.I’m very grateful to them.

Feeling of life

We don't understand the value of a moment until they become memories is my favorite quote.It’s gradually understanding in my life.Whenever we are in happiness or a happy situation, we don’t care about the value of the situation or the moment . When we lose、when we don’t have it again, we realize that the value of the situation or a moment , all we can do is to remember and miss,If I can, I very much hope that I will not lose anything.Facing the troubles and nostalgia in life, I often like to sit alone on the mountain and look at the distance.It is a moment of great happiness to settle down, let go of everything, and have a moment of relaxation.
I don’t want to write any more. It’s 1:30 now. Although this weekend, I still have to study…
